Privacy Policy – GymPal


Last updated: 28/03/2026

GymPal is designed to work offline: your programs and notebook stay on the device.

Data Collection

Workout-related data (programs, notebook, exercises, notes) is stored only on your device and is not sent to GymPal servers.
If crash reporting is enabled in the build you installed, the app may send error and stability reports through Sentry (sentry.io): for example stack traces, device model, OS version, and app version. By default, extra personal identifiers are not included.

Crash reporting and diagnostics

Stability reports are processed by Functional Software, Inc. (Sentry) on behalf of the developer. Details:
https://sentry.io/privacy/

In-app purchases

The app offers in-app purchases to unlock premium features.
Purchases are handled by Google Play and may involve data collection by Google, according to their privacy policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

GymPal does not have access to payment information.

Data Sharing

Your training content is not sold or shared with third parties for marketing. Crash and stability data may be processed by Sentry as described in their privacy policy.

Security

All data remains stored locally on the user’s device.
