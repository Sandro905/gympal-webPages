Privacy Policy – GymPal


Last updated: 28/03/2026

GymPal is designed to work completely offline. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Data Collection

The app does not collect or transmit any personal data.
All data (workouts, exercises, notes) is stored locally on the user's device.

In-app purchases

The app offers in-app purchases to unlock premium features.
Purchases are handled by Google Play and may involve data collection by Google, according to their privacy policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

GymPal does not have access to payment information.

Data Sharing

No user data is shared with third parties.

Security

All data remains stored locally on the user’s device.
